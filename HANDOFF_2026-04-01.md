## Watch out for

- **UI must be English.** Shechter doesn't speak French. An executor copied Fortress French text once — Alan was not happy.
- **No numeric scores in UI.** Show signal badges (Price drop, Reappeared, etc.) and classification labels (hot/warm/cold) — never the raw distress_score number.
- **React hooks before early returns.** This caused React error #310 three separate times. Any useState/useMutation MUST be before any `if (isLoading) return` guard.
- **Madlan images use `images2.madlan.co.il`** (not `images.madlan.co.il`). Was a bug.
- **Route ordering matters.** `/api/scan/all` must be registered BEFORE `/api/scan/{preset_id}` or FastAPI treats "all" as an integer.
- **Removal detection** only runs in `run_all_scans()`, not per-preset, to avoid false removals.
